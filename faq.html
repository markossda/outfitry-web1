Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Outfitry.

Outfitry is an AI-powered outfit generator app for iPhone that lets you upload a photo and generate different outfit variations.

Upload a photo, choose a style preset, and tap Generate. The AI creates outfit variations in seconds based on your preferences.

Outfitry is available for iPhone running iOS 15 or later. iPad support may be added in future updates.

Full-body photos with good lighting and plain backgrounds work best. Face the camera directly for optimal results.

Results may vary depending on photo quality and style selection. Try different photos or presets for better variations.

Processing speed depends on your internet connection and server load. Pro users get priority processing.
