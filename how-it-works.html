How It Works

Using Outfitry to change outfits in photos is simple: 1) Upload a full-body photo from your iPhone camera roll or take a new one, 2) Choose a style preset like Casual, Formal, Streetwear, or Athletic, 3) Tap Generate and wait a few seconds for AI to create outfit variations. Save your favorites to camera roll. Best results come from well-lit photos with plain backgrounds. The app is free to try with 10 generations.

Three simple steps to explore new outfit styles.

1

Upload Your Photo

Take a new photo or select one from your camera roll. Full-body photos with good lighting work best.

2

Choose a Style

Select from presets like Casual, Formal, Streetwear, or Athletic. The AI uses your preference to guide generation.

3

Generate & Save

Tap Generate and wait a few seconds. Browse the variations and save your favorites to camera roll.
