What Is an AI Outfit Changer?

An AI outfit changer uses artificial intelligence to modify clothing in photographs. These apps analyze your photo, understand body position, and generate new outfit variations while maintaining realistic proportions.

How It Works

  1. Image Analysis: AI identifies the person and clothing
  2. Segmentation: Separates person from background
  3. Style Input: You choose preferences
  4. Generation: AI generates new outfit imagery
  5. Output: Polished result with realistic lighting

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Download Outfitry from the App Store
  2. Upload your photo or take a new one
  3. Select a style preset
  4. Tap Generate and wait a few seconds
  5. Browse and save your favorites

Best Practices

  • Use full-body photos
  • Good, natural lighting
  • Face the camera directly
  • Plain backgrounds work best
  • Try multiple times for variety
