What Are AI Outfit Apps?

AI outfit apps use artificial intelligence to digitally change or generate clothing on photos. These apps analyze your photo, understand body positioning, and create realistic outfit variations. They're popular among fashion enthusiasts, content creators, and anyone curious about trying new styles without shopping.
