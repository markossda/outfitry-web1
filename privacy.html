Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Outfitry ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

  • Photos: Images you upload for outfit generation
  • Account Information: Email if you create an account
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about app usage
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status (via Apple)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the AI outfit generation service
  • Process and manage subscriptions
  • Improve the app experience
  • Respond to support requests

Photo Processing

Photos are processed to generate outfit variations. We:

  • Process photos securely
  • Do not use photos for AI training without consent
  • Allow you to delete your data anytime

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that help operate the app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

Your Rights

You may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Object to certain processing

Contact support@outfitry.app to exercise these rights.

Children's Privacy

Outfitry is not intended for children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Check the "Last updated" date.

Contact
